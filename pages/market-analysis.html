The System

What Was Actually Built

A production accounting system for Chubby Buttons LLC, an Amazon FBA + Shopify ecommerce brand. Every component is live and tested.

\uD83D\uDCCA

Amazon Settlement Automation

SP-API pulls settlement data, a webhook aggregates 16 fee categories, and Make.com creates an 11-line journal entry in QuickBooks every two weeks.

\uD83D\uDCE7

Vendor Invoice Processing

Gmail watchers monitor three inboxes. AI parses invoice details, Make.com creates draft Bills in QuickBooks with correct vendor and category.

\uD83C\uDFE6

Smart Bank Rules

16 QuickBooks rules auto-categorize recurring transactions across bank and credit card accounts. Covers subscriptions, deposits, payroll.

\uD83E\uDDE0

AI Financial Analysis

Claude AI interprets P&L data, flags anomalies, calculates margins, compares trends, and answers follow-up questions in real time.

Compliance

Legal, Licensing, and Security

No CPA License Required

Bookkeeping services do not require a government-issued license in the US. You build automations, not practice accounting. E&O insurance recommended (~$500-$1K/yr).

Client Data Security

Clients grant QBO admin access (same as any bookkeeper). API credentials stay under their accounts. No bank passwords shared. Simple data handling agreement covers scope.

Optional Certifications

CPB credential from NACPB adds credibility but is not required. QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification is free and signals legitimacy.

Works Alongside CPAs

This handles bookkeeping (recording transactions). Tax prep still requires a CPA. Position as complement, not replacement.
