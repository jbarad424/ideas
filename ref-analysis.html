Why this page exists: Look at the reference photos. Find what the winners have in common. Then shoot similar photos for Tron (and any future colorway) and we're done. Justin's eyes are better than mine for spotting the pattern, so I'm not going to tell you what I think it is — I'm going to show you the data.

Each card shows: the original reference photo on the left, the best-known production gen from that ref on the right (if any), and a one-line verdict from session data. Cards are grouped by status.

What to look for: camera angle, crop tightness, how much of the strap is visible, whether the model is wearing the device over a jacket or bare skin, device orientation on the arm, background clutter, lighting direction. Any of those could be the deciding factor.
