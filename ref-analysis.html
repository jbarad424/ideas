CB2 Reference Photo Meta-Analysis

April 8 2026 · 12 A/B refs · Each ref shown with all gens it produced this session

Why this page exists: Compare each reference photo to the actual outputs it produced. Find what the winners have in common. Find what the killed ones have in common. Then shoot similar photos for Tron and any future colorway.

Each block: reference photo on the left, all gens produced by that ref on the right. Every gen from every test this session — A/B rotated, V1 batch, dual-ref, snow tests, and mirror tests. Total gens per ref ranges from 4 to 20.

What to look for in the ref photos: camera angle, crop tightness, lighting direction, whether the device is on a bare arm or over a jacket, how much of the strap is visible, background clutter, device orientation relative to forearm, how clear the buttons are in the ref itself.
