Proven Findings
Waist-up framing = sweet spotCONFIRMED
Full body loses product (too small, duplicates). Extreme close-up loses scene context. Waist-up/3/4 body is the right balance.
R3 full-body: "horrible, floating, superimposed." R4 waist-up: "product looks awesome", "pretty damn good."
3 refs >>> 1 refCONFIRMED
Multiref with 3 reference images dramatically outperforms single-reference approaches (Kontext, flux-2-pro-preview).
R1 multiref: "nailed it." R2 single-ref: "lighting fake, LEDs wrong direction, too close to wrist."
Simple prompts > verbose promptsCONFIRMED
"This exact black remote with 5 buttons on forearm" beats long detailed descriptions of every product feature.
The best-rated R1 images used the simplest prompts. Over-specification seems to confuse the model.
