Completed Phases

Phase 0: Discovery and Planning Complete
Mapped HCA's entire manual workflow, identified automation opportunities, built the roadmap.
Phase 1: QuickBooks Foundation Complete
Designed and implemented 15 bank rules to auto-categorize recurring transactions (Shopify, Amazon, insurance, subscriptions, ads).
Phase 2: Amazon Settlement Pipeline Complete
Python webhook on Render pulls Amazon SP-API settlement data, aggregates fees, and posts balanced journal entries to QuickBooks. Includes deduplication via Data Store.
Phase 3: Vendor Invoice Capture Complete
Email watchers on 3 inboxes auto-parse incoming invoices with AI and create bills in QuickBooks.
Phase 4: QuickBooks Bank Rules Complete
15 of 16 rules live in QBO. Layer 1 of the 3-layer categorization system.
Phase 5: Financial Package Generator Complete
17-tab Excel workbook generated from live QuickBooks data. Includes Dashboard, P&L, Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, reconciliation tabs for Amazon/PayPal/Shopify clearing, bank recon, credit card recon, inventory, and full general ledger. Deployed on Render, triggered by approval gate.
Phase 6A: Exception Queue and Monthly Close Hub Complete
Notion-based Exception Queue for transactions the AI can't categorize. Monthly Close Checklist with 8 prerequisite steps and a human approval gate before the Financial Package is generated.
Phase 6B: AI Transaction Review Complete
Claude AI scans QuickBooks daily for uncategorized transactions. High confidence categorizations are applied directly. Low confidence items get flagged to the Exception Queue with a plain English question for the owners.
Phase 6C: Inventory Tracking Complete
Ron Hawk's warehouse inventory counts are captured from email and recorded in Notion automatically.
Phase 6D: Shopify Connector Complete
Native Intuit Shopify connector enabled in QuickBooks. Orders, fees, taxes, and payouts sync automatically.
