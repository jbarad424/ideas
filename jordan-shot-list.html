CB2 Reference Photo Shot List

For Jordan — April 2026 — Takes ~30 minutes total

What This Is For

We're using AI to generate lifestyle marketing photos of the CB2 on people doing sports. The AI needs a reference photo to know what the CB2 looks like. The reference photo also controls what clothing the person wears in the generated image — so if the ref shows a T-shirt, the AI generates T-shirts. We need refs showing sport-appropriate gear.

KEY RULE: These are NOT action shots. You do NOT need to be doing the sport. Just wearing the right clothing. A parking lot in a ski jacket works perfectly — the AI creates the scene.

Photo Requirements (Every Shot)

PERSPECTIVE: Another person takes the photo. NOT looking down at your own arm. The photographer stands in front or to the side and photographs the CB2 on your forearm.
  1. CB2 on the forearm, BELOW the elbow — positioned between the wrist and elbow, closer to mid-forearm
  2. Button icons clearly visible — all 5 symbols must be readable
  3. LED circle with frosted plastic intact — the little circle above the + button must be visible with the plastic cover on
  4. Strap with Chubby Buttons logo visible
  5. Waist-up framing — show the arm + upper body at minimum
  6. Natural lighting — outdoor or well-lit indoor, no harsh flash
  7. Shoot BOTH arms: CB2 on left arm → 2-3 photos → swap to right arm → 2-3 photos
USE UPDATED UNITS ONLY: Units must have fresh symbol paint with bright outlines and the frosted LED plastic circle intact. The old Hunter units with faded symbols have been causing problems in AI generation.
