What We're Building

CB Creative Lab is an internal tool that uses multiple AI image and video generation engines to create marketing assets for Chubby Buttons. Instead of expensive photo shoots or generic stock imagery, we generate custom content trained on what we like — then iterate until it's ad-ready.

The Problem

We need a constant stream of fresh marketing visuals — product shots, action sports photography, lifestyle content, and video — but professional shoots are expensive and stock photos don't feature our actual product. AI generation is powerful but generic. It doesn't know what Chubby Buttons looks like or what our brand aesthetic is.

The Solution

We feed AI engines actual CB2 product photos as reference, generate content across multiple engines, and then rate the results. The structured feedback loops back into better prompts. Over time, we discover and save "styles" that work — and generation becomes one-tap.

How It Works

1

Generate

Claude sends prompts + CB2 reference photos to FLUX, Recraft, and Runway. Each engine produces different styles. Images and video land in Google Drive automatically.

2

Rate

Founders open the CB Creative Lab app and rate each image on two dimensions: product accuracy (does it look like real CB2?) and scene/vibe (is the composition, lighting, and setting right?). Structured feedback with specific reasons.

3

Learn

Claude analyzes the ratings — which prompts worked, which engines performed best, which scenes resonated. Each batch gets progressively better as the system learns what "on-brand" means for CB.

4

Save Styles

When a winning combination emerges (lighting + scene + angle that everyone loves), it gets saved as a named "style" — like "Epic Winter Action" or "Clean Studio Product." Future generation starts from these proven templates.

5

One-Tap Create

Eventually: pick a colorway (Tron/Hunter/Patriot), pick a saved style, describe the scene briefly, and get back a batch of polished images + video. No prompt engineering needed.

The Engines

Images

FLUX

Black Forest Labs. Best for photorealistic action shots and product placement using reference photos.

Images

Recraft

Purpose-built for commercial imagery. Clean product shots, editorial layouts, and marketing-ready visuals.

Video

Runway

Industry-standard AI video. Takes a still image and brings it to life — 5-second cinematic clips from a single photo.

Same prompt goes to multiple engines. You rate which output you prefer. The best engine for each style emerges naturally.
